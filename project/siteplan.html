Site Plan - Focus First

Beating study procrastination

Site Name

FocusFirst

I chose this name because it sums up the goal of the site: putting Focus first. It is short, easy to remember, and directly communicates the idea of starting to study and staying on task, which is exactly the problem the site helps solve.

Optional domain: focusfirst-study.org

Site Purpose

FocusFirst gives students practical information, based on psychology and behavioral analysis, about why we procrastinate on schoolwork and how to start studying and stay focused. Beyond the explanatory content, the site offers interactive tools: a quiz that identifies the user's main procrastination type and gives personalized advice, and a study-task tracker that saves progress between visits using localStorage. There is also a contact/feedback page where students can share their struggles and suggest study tips.

Scenarios

Questions a visitor from the target audience might ask:

  • Why do I always put off studying until the last minute, and what can I do to start now?
  • What is my main procrastination type, and which study tips work best for me?
  • How can I track my assignments and see my progress without losing the data I close the site?

Color Schema

The following colors were selected and are applied in this document:

  • Teal (#1f6f78): header, navigation, and section headings.
  • Mint green (#5bbf9e): accents, highlight borders, and buttons.
  • Light gray (#f4f7f6): page background color.
  • Dark gray (#2b2b2b): body text (paragraphs).

Typography

The selected fonts are also applied in this document:

  • Poppins: used for all headings and titles (h1, h2).
  • Open Sans: used for body text, paragraphs, and lists.
